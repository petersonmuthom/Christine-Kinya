About
Me
I am Christine Kinya, an accomplished Kenyan model, pageant queen, event director, and mentor in the fashion and beauty industry. I combine beauty, elegance, and leadership, inspiring upcoming talent through my journey and achievements. My modeling career is marked by prestigious titles, including : Miss MIBS 2023 ( Meru Institute of Business Studies), 1st Runner-Up Miss Meru County 2023, 1st Runner-Up Miss Empowerment Meru 2022.

ExperienceMiss MIBS.
Crowned Miss Meru Institute of Business Studies (MIBS) 2022- 2023, Miss Meru County 1st Runner- Up 2022- 2023, and Miss Empowerment Meru 1st Runner-Up 2021- 2022, I have always carried my titles as a call to serve. My reigns were more than moments on stage; they became opportunities to create impact beyond the runway.
Director, EL-Duce Entertainment.
As Director of EL-duce Entertainment, I brought this vision to life by organizing Mr & Miss Heritage Meru on 12th October 2024, an unforgettable evening that celebrated culture, elegance, and unity. The event showcased the richness of Meru’s heritage while providing a platform for young talent to shine.
Judge at high-profile events.
My journey also extends to nurturing and recognizing talent as a judge at high-profile events such as Mr & Miss Kiirua Technical Training Institute 2024, Mr & Miss Chuka Vocational Training Institute 2024, Miss Kirigara Girls High School 2025, and Miss Gikumene Girls High School 2025. In these roles, I have been privileged to witness the courage, dedication, and artistry of contestants, helping to guide the next generation of titleholders.
Charity events.
I have proudly attended and supported various charity events, using my platform to champion causes that matter, from uplifting vulnerable communities to raising awareness on social issues. To me, a crown is not just a symbol of beauty, but a promise to lead with purpose.
Creative Collaborations.
Beyond pageantry, I explore the creative world through collaborations, including a visual storytelling project with the gifted photographer Cliff Creatives. This project captured not just images, but emotions, culture, and the essence of my journey — proof that art, beauty, and purpose can coexist in powerful harmony.Through every title, event, and collaboration, my goal remains the same: to inspire, empower, and leave a legacy of beauty that serves.
